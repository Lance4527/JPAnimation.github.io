Shonen Overview

The category of anime directed at teens about a story of someone who goes on an adventure and grows in order to accomplish their end goal. The most well known genre with common household names like Naruto or Dragon Ball Z.

Naruto

An anime with near 1,000 episodes. It follows our untalented and hated village child Naruto as he strives to become Hokage. Filled with many dramatic moments of mastering the beast within him, and trying to bring back his friend Sasuke who deserts the village in pursuit of revenge.

One Piece

An easy going pirate named Luffy sets out to sea in the footsteps of the pirate Shanks who saved him when he was a boy. While his intellect is never going to win him any awards, his loyalty and trust in his friends pulls everyone together no matter how dire the situation. He sails the sea in hopes of surpassing Shanks and obtaining the One Piece in order to become the king of pirates!

My Hero Academia

Midoriya is one of the very few people who does not have a quirk, which are super powers and in this world almost everyone has one. While lacking powers he does not lack in courage despite being timid and weak. As if chosen by fate he meets a hero who has idolized his whole life and is able to inherit his power. He then strives to become the number one hero and stop the recent uprising of the league of villians.
