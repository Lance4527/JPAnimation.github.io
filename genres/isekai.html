Isekai Overview

At some point we may all have pondered what life was like living inside our favorite games or shows. Isekai is the embodiment of that notion. The protaganists enviroment ranges from a world where the outcome of a game decides every decision in life, to fighting to protect people as a summoned hero, but they always start in mediocre lives as a student or corporate employee. Here I've listed a few of the best.

The Rising Of The Shield Hero

Naofumi is a regular college student who lives a regular life studying while his living expenses are paid for. Another regular day at the library and he picks up a strange looking book about 4 heroes. When we flips to the section about the shield hero he's transported to another world. Not your typical protagonist origin, as the shield hero is hated in this world which leads to him being set up and exiled. Watch Naofumi's rise as the shield hero!

No Game No Life

Living in a world where nothing is as simple as they are in games, sibling Sora and Shiro stick to being the best gaming duo known as blank. Amongst their gaming a mysterious email invites them to a game of chess. After winning the game they are asked the strange question if they should have been born in a different world where games dictate all. To their surprise when they answer "yes" they are transported to another world where games dictate everything and not even crime is possible. Sora and Shiro begin their conquest of this world in order to challenge the god of this mysterious world!

Re:Zero - Starting Life in Another World

Subaru is a completely average, if not slightly underaverage person. After a small run to the gas station he finds himself in another world. Realizing he must have some kind of powers as someone summoned to another world, he tries to do what you would expect. However, after failing to use any kind of magic, failing to rescue a child, and getting beat up in an alley, he then becomes very confused as to why he is even in this world. He follows a girl that saves him and develops some feelings and decides to help her no matter what. He then realizes what his power in this world is after he's killed and comes back to life in the previous day.
