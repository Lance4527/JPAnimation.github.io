Isn't Anime just cartoons?

Anime is typically seen as the same as cartoons in western culture. This is because in our culture all of our shows for the most part are live action and anything animated is usually reserved for children. This is different from anime because in Japan they simply use animation as their their choice for entertainment and there's many genre for different age and genders.

It's okay if you end up obsessing and only watching anime every day, as said by Roronoa Zoro:

“When I decided to follow my dream, I had already discarded my life.”

Genres of Anime and Who They're Directed To

  • Seinen

    Seinen is a genre of anime directed at young adult men. These include political focused anime or anime that is very intense and usually rated M.

  • Shonen

    The genre directed at teens is Shonen. These include your classics such as Naruto or Dragon Ball Z, but times have also changed and there's plenty more to name.

  • Isekai

    This genre focuses on fiction. Ever wonder what life would be like if you ended up in a game like world? That what Isekai is all about.
